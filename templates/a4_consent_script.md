# A4 Option 4 — Consent Script (read or send before the interview)

> Use verbatim or tighten; do not weaken. The participant must actively agree before the interview starts; record the agreement in your field notes (no signatures needed at this scale).

---

Thanks for helping with my course project. Before we start, five things you should know:

1. **You'll be interviewed by an AI**, not by me. I built it for a course assignment and I'm testing how well it interviews — *it* is being evaluated, not you.
2. The topic is **[TOPIC]** — nothing personal or sensitive. If any question feels off, skip it; no reason needed.
3. It takes about **10–15 minutes**. You can **stop at any time**, also without giving a reason.
4. I'll use the **anonymized transcript** in my coursework: your name and any identifying details are removed before anyone (including the instructor) sees it. The transcript may pass through an AI service as part of the interview itself.
5. Afterwards I'll ask you **three quick rating questions** about the experience (2 minutes).

Are you happy to go ahead?

---

*After the interview:* Please rate, 1 (not at all) to 5 (very much): **(a)** I felt listened to. **(b)** The questions felt relevant and well-timed. **(c)** I would have said roughly the same things to a human interviewer. — And in one sentence: what stood out, good or bad?
